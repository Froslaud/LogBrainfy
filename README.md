## Account Deletion

Users have full control over their data within LogBrainfy. All areas of the application—including notes, tasks, projects, habits, areas, and lists—can be edited or deleted at any time by the user.

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at:

📧 froslaud@gmail.com

Upon receiving your request, we will process the account deletion in accordance with applicable data protection regulations. Once the account is deleted, all associated personal data and user-generated content will be removed from our systems, except where retention is required by law.

Please note:

- Data stored locally on your device can also be removed by uninstalling the application or deleting content manually.
- Synchronization data (if applicable) will be removed from our servers after account deletion.
- The deletion process is irreversible.
